Neil Gaiman

Author and WordSmith

Neil Gaiman, born November 10, 1960 in Portchester, England, UK and is an author of short fiction, novels, comic books, graphic novels, audio theatre, and films. He is best known for the comic book series The Sandman, Stardust, American Gods, Coraline, and The Graveyard Book. He holds various prestigious awards including the Hugo, Nebula, and Bram Stoker awards, as well as the Newbery and Carnegie medals. He has also worked with
